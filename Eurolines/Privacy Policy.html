FAQs:

How do I change or cancel my booking?
Passengers who booked a trip using the FlixBus website or apps can manage their booking via the “Manage My Booking" platform. Here you can easily manage your booking and travel details and download the boarding pass.

How do I reach customer service?
Passengers who booked a trip using the FlixBus website or apps, or are planning to book a trip, can reach customer service here.

What does my bus look like?
If you’ve booked a FlixBus ride, these are operated by the typical green FlixBuses or non-branded buses by FlixBus partners.

Where can I book a ticket and where can I get information on the bus schedule and bus stop?
Passengers can get information and book their ticket for travel via the FlixBus website or mobile app.
